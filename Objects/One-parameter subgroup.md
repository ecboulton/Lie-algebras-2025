### Definition
A **one-parameter subgroup** of a Lie group $G$ is a Lie group homomorphism $\phi: \mathbb{R}^+ \to G$.
i.e. smooth and $\phi(s+t)=\phi(s)\phi(t)$.
Such a homomorphism exists for any Lie group.
