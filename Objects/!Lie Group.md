### Definition
A **Lie group** is a group $G$ which is also a smooth manifold such that the group multiplication
$$
\begin{align}
 \mu: G \times G & \to G  \\
 (a,b) & \mapsto ab
\end{align}
$$
and inverse operation
$$
\begin{align}
 i: G & \to G \\
a & \mapsto a^{-1}
\end{align}
$$
are smooth ($C^\infty$).
